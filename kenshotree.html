KenshoTree

The Challenge

KenshoTree is a website that matches clients with leadership, management, and wellness coaches. Our challenge was to redesign the current client onboarding process to be scalabe and increase conversions.

My Role

  • UX Research

  • Interaction Design

Process & Deliverables

In collaboration with my teammate Rachel, my role included the following:
Domain analysis, competitive analysis, user interviews, SME interviews, usability testing, user personas, wireframes and mid-fidelity prototypes.

Research

Our design process included domain analysis and competitive analysis where we examined current processes for matching coaches and clients. Based on insights from SME interviews, we also examined algorithm-based online dating sites to learn how these sites determine compatibility between two people.

Following our research synthesis we developed user personas, our problem statement, and design principles.

Problem Statement:
How might we improve the matching process so that it provides more compatible results.

Wireframes & Prototyping

Following multiple ideation sessions, I created a low-fidelity prototype which we used to perform usability testing. Following the usability tests we clarified the language in the prototype and modified the layout of the Search Results page.

Our final wireframes allow the client to determine primary search criteria and then use filters to select secondary criteria and see the results dynamically change based on their selections. The search process also allows coaches to be tagged as 'favourites' who can be emailed to a third party. Also, the coach profile page includes two videos which help the client assess compatibility.
